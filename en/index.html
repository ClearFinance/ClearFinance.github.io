About · Sobre Nosotros

Why
Clear Finance?

Too many people make critical decisions about their money — buying a home, investing, taking on debt, starting a business — with incomplete, confusing or biased information. Clear Finance exists to change that.

This blog is built from both academia and practice — from training in Business Administration and International Finance & Business, and from the constant study of markets, the global economy and the realities of Latin American entrepreneurship.

Our promise is simple: rigorous content, clear language and no conflicts of interest. Here there are no financial products to sell and no commissions to earn. Just information that helps you make better decisions.
